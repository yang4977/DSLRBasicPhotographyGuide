Focusing the Camera Lens

No matter what type of camera lens you choose to use, it is important to understand how to properly focus your frame!

  1. Shift the lens left/right to focus in/out
  2. Point the camera lens toward the intended subject
  3. Lightly press down the shutter button until the red box on the display screen turns green. If the red box turns green, that indicates the camera lens is focused on the frame.
  4. Press the shutter button all the way down to capture the photo! (You should hear a clicking sound)
